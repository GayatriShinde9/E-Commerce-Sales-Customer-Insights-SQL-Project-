# E-Commerce Sales & Customer Insights (SQL Project)
## Project Overview
### This project analyzes an E-Commerce sales database using SQL to extract meaningful business insights.
### The database includes customers, products, orders, and order details, allowing analysis of:
#### •	Revenue performance
#### •	Customer behavior
#### •	Product profitability
#### •	Sales trends
#### •	Category-wise performance
### The project demonstrates strong SQL fundamentals and real-world business analysis skills.

## Project Objectives
#### •	Calculate total revenue and monthly sales growth
#### •	Identify top-selling and most profitable products
#### •	Segment customers based on purchase behavior
#### •	Calculate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
#### •	Measure Average Order Value (AOV)
#### •	Analyze revenue by product category

## Database Schema
## The database consists of four relational tables:
### 1️Customers
#### Column	Description
#### customer_id	Unique customer ID
#### first_name	Customer first name
#### last_name	Customer last name
#### email	Email address
#### city	City
#### state	State
#### signup_date	Registration date

## 2.Products
#### Column	Description
#### product_id	Unique product ID
#### product_name	Product name
#### category	Product category
#### price	Selling price
#### cost	Product cost

## 3.Orders
#### Column	Description
#### order_id	Unique order ID
#### customer_id	Foreign key (Customers)
#### order_date	Order date
#### total_amount	Total order value

## 4.Order Details
#### Column	Description
#### order_detail_id	Unique record ID
#### order_id	Foreign key (Orders)
#### product_id	Foreign key (Products)
#### quantity	Quantity purchased
#### price	Selling price per unit

## Technologies Used
#### •	SQL (MySQL)
#### •	Relational Database Design
#### •	Joins (INNER JOIN, LEFT JOIN)
#### •	Aggregate Functions (SUM, COUNT, AVG)
#### •	GROUP BY & ORDER BY
#### •	CASE Statements
#### •	Profit Calculations

 ## Key Analysis Performed
### Total Revenue
#### Calculated total sales across all orders.
### Monthly Sales Trend
#### Analyzed month-wise revenue to identify growth patterns.
### Top-Selling Products
#### Identified products with highest quantity sold.
### Revenue by Category
#### Analyzed category-level sales performance.
### Top Customers
#### Identified high-value customers based on total spending.
### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
#### Calculated total revenue generated by each customer.
### Average Order Value (AOV)
#### Measured average revenue per order.
### Most Profitable Products
#### Calculated profit using:
#### Profit = (Selling Price - Cost) × Quantity

## Business Insights
#### •	Electronics category contributes the highest revenue.
#### •	Premium products increase Average Order Value.
#### •	Repeat customers generate higher lifetime value.
#### •	A small percentage of customers contribute a large portion of revenue.
#### •	Certain months show higher sales growth, indicating seasonal trends.
